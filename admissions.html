Admissions

Eligibility Criteria

  • Students must meet government selection standards for regular admission.
  • Completion of primary school and passing the Malawi National Primary School Certificate Examination (PSE) for Form 1 entry.
  • ODEL (Open Distance Education Learning) students must also have passed the Primary School Leaving Certificate (PSLC) exams for Form 1 entry.
  • ODEL students need to complete a special enrollment form before being admitted.
  • Commitment to uphold school rules, discipline, and values.

Application Process

  1. Complete the admission form (available for download below).
  2. Attach certified copies of primary school certificates or other relevant documents.
  3. Submit the application to the school administration office or via email.
  4. Wait for government selection approval or ODEL enrollment confirmation.
