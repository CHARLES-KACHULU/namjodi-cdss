Student Life

School Clubs & Societies

Students actively participate in clubs such as Science Club, Debate Society, Drama, and Music Club. These clubs encourage leadership, teamwork, and creativity.

Excursions & Trips

Our students and teachers often travel for educational trips, cultural exchanges, and sports events. For example, students visited Lilongwe National Park to study wildlife conservation.

Visitor Highlights

Visitors from Govan School, Scotland, visited Namjodi-abc Secondary School. They donated stationeries and contributed fees for some students, enhancing learning opportunities for all.

Sports & Competitions

Students engage in football, netball, athletics, and inter-school competitions. Sports participation develops teamwork, discipline, and a healthy lifestyle.

Cultural & Extracurricular Activities

Namjodi-abc students take part in drama, music, debate, and art competitions. These activities enhance creativity, self-expression, and critical thinking.

Community Service

Students engage in community service programs like environmental cleanups, mentoring younger students, and local charity initiatives. These programs build social responsibility and empathy.

Highlights

  • Students excel in academics and extracurricular competitions.
  • Exchange programs and educational trips.
  • Active participation in community service projects.
  • Visitors and donors contributing to student welfare.
