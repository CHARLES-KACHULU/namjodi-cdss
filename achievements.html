Achievements

Academic Excellence

Namjodi-abc Secondary School students consistently achieve top results in national examinations and competitions. Many have received scholarships to prestigious schools and universities.

Sports Achievements

Our students excel in football, netball, athletics, and other sports competitions. They regularly bring home trophies from inter-school and regional tournaments.

Arts & Cultural Awards

Namjodi-abc students participate in drama, music, art, and debate competitions. Their creativity and talents have earned recognition at district and national levels.

Community & Leadership

Students engage in leadership programs, environmental initiatives, and community service, receiving awards for their contributions to society.
