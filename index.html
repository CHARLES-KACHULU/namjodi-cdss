Principal's Welcome

Namjodi-abc Secondary School is a government institution committed to academic excellence and holistic development. Our mission is to nurture responsible citizens equipped with knowledge, skills, and values to thrive in a competitive world.

Students engage in co-curricular and extracurricular activities that foster leadership, creativity, teamwork, and critical thinking.
