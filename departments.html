Departments

Science Department

The Science Department includes Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Computer Studies. Students are engaged in practical experiments, laboratory work, and ICT integration to enhance understanding of scientific principles.

Mathematics Department

Our Mathematics Department focuses on problem-solving, analytical thinking, and preparing students for national examinations and competitions. Innovative teaching methods and group activities are encouraged.

Languages Department

The Languages Department covers English, Chichewa, and other languages. Emphasis is placed on communication skills, literature appreciation, writing, and reading comprehension.

Humanities Department

This department includes History, Geography, and Social Life Skills. Students explore cultural, social, and environmental topics while developing critical thinking and civic responsibility.

Business & Arts Department

Includes Business Studies, Art, and Music. Students develop entrepreneurial skills, creative talents, and participate in events that showcase innovation and artistic expression.

Co-curricular Activities

Departments organize various competitions, exhibitions, field trips, and community projects to supplement classroom learning and enhance students' holistic development.
