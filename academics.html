Academics

Curriculum & Subjects

Namjodi-abc Secondary School follows the government-approved secondary school curriculum. Students study core subjects including English, Mathematics, Science, Chichewa, History, Geography, Business Studies, Computer Studies, and other electives according to their stream and level.

Teaching & Learning

The school emphasizes interactive and practical learning. Laboratories, ICT rooms, libraries, and teaching aids are used to ensure students understand concepts effectively and develop critical thinking skills.

Co-curricular Activities

Academic growth is complemented by co-curricular activities. Students participate in debates, clubs, drama, sports, music, and community service projects that develop leadership, teamwork, and creativity.

Achievements

The school consistently achieves high academic results. Students have won awards in regional and national competitions in science, mathematics, sports, and cultural events.

Students and parents can download important academic materials, including timetables, syllabus, and other school resources for each term.
