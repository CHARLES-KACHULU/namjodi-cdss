Namjodi-abc Secondary School

About Us

Namjodi-abc Secondary School is a government institution located in Lilongwe, Malawi. Students are admitted through government selection. The school emphasizes both academic excellence and character development, ensuring that students are prepared for higher education and responsible citizenship.

The teaching staff is dedicated to nurturing students‚Äô talents, ethical values, leadership, and social skills. Holistic growth is encouraged through sports, arts, debates, and cultural programs.

The school has well-equipped classrooms, laboratories, a computer lab, library, and sports fields. Co-curricular activities, mentorship programs, and educational trips provide students with exposure beyond the classroom.

Core Values

  • Integrity and Honesty
  • Academic Excellence
  • Discipline and Respect
  • Teamwork and Leadership
  • Community Service

History

Established in the early 1980s, Namjodi-abc Secondary School has grown to become one of the leading government schools in the region. Our alumni have excelled in various professions and continue to contribute positively to society.

Visitors & Community Engagement

Namjodi-abc has hosted several local and international visitors. For example, students and staff from Govan School in Scotland visited our school, donated stationeries, and supported the education of selected students.

Students and teachers have also traveled to participate in educational programs and cultural exchange activities. Community partnerships are actively encouraged to enhance the learning experience.
